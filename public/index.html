When is a Freeze Dryer not a Dehydrator?

I have a theory I intend to explore. These “Freeze Dryers” ($$$). that people are buying, are really a “Class” of Dehydrators; a class that does NOT use HEAT to dehydrate. A good example is: In my younger days, when we use to help Mum with the laundry (in the winter). Back in my generation, we use to use a clothesline, and we use to hang laundry out to dry. In the (very) cold weather, the laundry use to freeze to the clothes line.
